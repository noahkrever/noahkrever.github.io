Is Quasar PG1302 A Binary Black Hole System?

Supermassive black hole system PG1302 appears periodic at optical wavelengths. I am leading an investigation into PG1302's periodicity by performing a likelihood analysis and comparing different noise/signal models. Does evidence for periodicity characteristic of a binary system increase or decrease with continued observations?
